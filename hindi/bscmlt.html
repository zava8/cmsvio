    FAQs

    What are BSc MLT core subjects?

    The BSc MLT course subjects include subjects such as Fundamentals of Medical Laboratory Technology, Human Anatomy, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Human Physiology, etc.

    What are the passing marks for B.Sc MLT?

    The candidate must secure atleast 35% marks in theory and practical examination individually in each subject.

    Does pursuing a BSc in MLT make you a doctor?

    No, a Bachelor of Science in Medical Laboratory Technology is a professional undergraduate course for Medical Lab Technicians or paramedics.

    Is BSc MLT hard or easy?

    The course requires passion and hard work to learn the diagnostic techniques, Laboratory results, equipment, medical conditions, etc. The course is considered an easy one comparatively and is one of the best to get into the healthcare industry.

    What is the focus of BSc MLT subjects in first year?

    BSc MLT subjects first year focus on understanding of topics like anatomy and physiology, biochemistry, microbiology, hematology, and pathology.

    Is there maths in BSc MLT?

    No, the B.Sc MLT course curriculum does not include mathematics.

    Is BSc MLT better than Nursing?

    The BSc MLT course curriculum is only restricted to medical lab technology and diagnostic studies whereas BSc Nursing courses focus on all aspects of healthcare including patient care, medical procedures, assessment, diagnosis and management.
